About

As a Web Developer, I develop web UI/UX design requirements for various projects, mainly using technologies such as React.js, Next.js and Redux. I integrate APIs, endpoints, and solve issues related to styles and functionalities. I have more than 8 years of experience as a Software Developer, working with different languages and frameworks, such as PHP, JavaScript, TypeScript, Tailwind, Laravel, and Chakra UI.

I graduated from Universidad Nacional Experimental Politécnica de la Fuerza Armada with a degree in System Engineering, where I learned about programming, network technology, and system analysis. I am committed to delivering quality work and open to new challenges. I have strategic soft skills that allow me to take responsibility for decision-making, leadership, and teamwork.
